# InvoiceFlow — Demo Video Recording Script

**Duration:** 2 minutes (120 seconds)
**Resolution:** 1080p minimum (1920×1080)
**Recording Tool:** [Loom](https://www.loom.com) (recommended) or [OBS Studio](https://obsproject.com)
**Format:** MP4, screen recording with voiceover
**Mic:** Use a decent USB mic or headset — clear audio matters more than fancy visuals
**Browser:** Chrome (clean profile, no extensions visible, bookmarks bar hidden)

---

## Pre-Recording Checklist

- [ ] Close all unnecessary browser tabs and desktop apps
- [ ] Hide bookmarks bar (`Cmd+Shift+B` / `Ctrl+Shift+B`)
- [ ] Use a clean Chrome profile (no password managers, extensions visible)
- [ ] Set browser zoom to 100%
- [ ] Test audio levels — speak at normal conversational volume
- [ ] Disable desktop notifications (DND mode)
- [ ] Open all pages in tabs beforehand for smooth transitions
- [ ] Have a demo account logged in and ready
- [ ] If using real data, blur/sanitize sensitive client information

---

## Script

---

### 0:00 – 0:10 | Landing Page & Hero Section

**🖥️ ON SCREEN:**
Show the InvoiceFlow landing page. Start from the top. Slowly scroll through the hero section showing the headline, subheadline, and CTA button. Pause briefly on the hero.

**🎙️ VOICEOVER:**
"InvoiceFlow is an AI-powered invoicing and billing platform built for freelancers, agencies, and small businesses. In this quick two-minute walkthrough, I'll show you exactly what's included — the full feature set, the UI, and how easy it is to manage your entire billing workflow from one dashboard."

---

### 0:10 – 0:30 | Pricing Page

**🖥️ ON SCREEN:**
Click on "Pricing" in the navigation. Show all three pricing tiers side by side. Scroll down slowly so each plan is clearly visible — Starter, Professional, and Enterprise. Highlight the feature comparison briefly.

**🎙️ VOICEOVER:**
"Here's the pricing page. There are three plans designed to scale with your business. The Starter plan covers freelancers who need basic invoicing. Professional adds client management, analytics, and payment tracking — ideal for growing agencies. And Enterprise includes multi-team support, API access, and priority features. Everything is clearly laid out, so users know exactly what they're getting at each tier."

---

### 0:30 – 0:50 | Dashboard Overview

**🖥️ ON SCREEN:**
Navigate to the logged-in dashboard. Show the full dashboard view — key metric cards at the top (total revenue, pending invoices, paid invoices, active clients). Pan down slowly to show the recent invoices table and the quick-action buttons.

**🎙️ VOICEOVER:**
"This is the main dashboard — your command center for everything. At the top, you get instant visibility into total revenue, pending payments, paid invoices, and your active client count. Below that, there's a recent invoices table so you can quickly see what's outstanding. The sidebar gives you one-click access to every section: invoices, clients, analytics, and settings."

---

### 0:50 – 1:10 | Create an Invoice (Full Flow)

**🖥️ ON SCREEN:**
Click "New Invoice" or the "+" button. Show the invoice creation form. Type in (or use pre-filled data): client name, invoice number, line items with description and amount. Show the "Add Line Item" button. Click through to the preview or save step. Show the PDF/download option.

**🎙️ VOICEOVER:**
"Creating an invoice takes less than a minute. Fill in the client details, add your line items — you can add as many as you need — set the due date, and you're done. You can preview the invoice, download it as a PDF, or send it directly to your client via email. The whole process is designed to be fast, so you spend less time on admin and more time on actual work."

---

### 1:10 – 1:25 | Client Management

**🖥️ ON SCREEN:**
Navigate to the "Clients" section. Show the client list/table. Click on one client to show the client detail view — contact info, invoice history, total billed, payment status. Show the "Add New Client" button briefly.

**🎙️ VOICEOVER:**
"The client management page keeps all your customer information organized in one place. Each client profile shows their contact details, full invoice history, and a summary of amounts billed versus paid. You can quickly see who your top clients are and who has outstanding balances. Adding a new client is just a click away."

---

### 1:25 – 1:45 | Analytics & Reports

**🖥️ ON SCREEN:**
Navigate to the "Analytics" section. Show the revenue chart (monthly/weekly toggle). Show the breakdown charts — revenue by client, invoice status distribution, payment trends. Scroll through the charts smoothly.

**🎙️ VOICEOVER:**
"The analytics page gives you real-time insights into your business financials. You can view monthly revenue trends, see which clients generate the most income, and track your invoice status distribution — how many are paid, pending, or overdue. These visual reports make it easy to understand your cash flow at a glance and make smarter business decisions."

---

### 1:45 – 2:00 | Settings + CTA

**🖥️ ON SCREEN:**
Navigate briefly to the Settings page. Show company profile / branding section. Then navigate back to the landing page or show the dashboard one final time. End with a clear CTA frame — either the pricing page or a "Buy Now" type message.

**🎙️ VOICEOVER:**
"In settings, users can customize their company profile, add a logo, configure tax details, and set up their payment preferences. Everything is clean, modern, and ready to use out of the box. InvoiceFlow comes with complete source code, documentation, and a straightforward setup process. If you're looking for a turnkey invoicing SaaS to launch, rebrand, or build upon — this is it. Thanks for watching, and feel free to reach out with any questions."

## Post-Recording Checklist

- [ ] Watch the full recording and check for:
  - [ ] Audio is clear and at consistent volume
  - [ ] No accidental screen flashes, cursor jumps, or loading delays
  - [ ] Text on screen is readable at 1080p
  - [ ] Transitions between pages are smooth (use browser tabs, not typing URLs)
  - [ ] Total duration is between 1:50 and 2:10
- [ ] Export as MP4 (H.264 codec) for maximum compatibility
- [ ] File size should be under 200MB for easy uploading
- [ ] Upload to YouTube (Unlisted) or embed directly in the listing

---

## Tips for a Polished Recording

| Tip | Details |
|-----|---------|
| **Pacing** | Don't rush. Pause for 1-2 seconds between sections. Silence is fine. |
| **Cursor** | Keep mouse movements slow and deliberate. Don't flick around. |
| **Clicks** | Use click indicators if Loom supports it — makes actions clear |
| **Mistakes** | If you mess up, pause for 3 seconds and restart that sentence. Edit later. |
| **Energy** | Sound interested and confident — buyers pick up on tone |
| **Length** | 2 minutes max. Buyers won't watch 5+ minutes. Keep it tight. |
| **Thumbnails** | Pick a frame showing the dashboard for the video thumbnail — looks professional |

---

*Record this in one clean take if possible. Minor edits are fine, but an authentic single-take feels more genuine to buyers.*
